CAPTION: New Gods

Chambara (el dios de la chamba)

"Also known as Ronn Chambara when he still walked among men as a mere mortal. Once a poet, he believes no great art can be achieved without pain and suffering. He now continues the same principals as a new god."

Valteil

Once a mortal man, Valteil sought godhood alongside his companions from the Fellowship. After ascending, he became the Enlightened One, a deity that represents mankind's desire for knowledge and enlightenment.
Now a New God, Valteil would devote his existence to the understanding and the creation of artificial life.

Francois

Once a mortal man, Francóis sought godhood alongside his companions from the Fellowship. After ascending, he became the Dominating One, a deity that represents mankind's desire for domination and conquest.
Now a New God, Francóis would conquer Ma'habre and sit atop the Throne of Ascension, preventing any intruders from becoming gods themselves.
